Membership

2026 Inaugural Season

For 2026, Old Colony Anglers is an informal, open-access club. There are no dues, no expectations, and no sign-up processes. We just ask that you join our Facebook group and come fish with us!

What's Next?

If local interest grows through the season, we're planning on introducing a free membership at the end of 2026 as a way to keep a roster, and plan merch orders.
This will also help us if we request access to private waters in the area, and to get an accurate headcount for future events.

Paid Membership

There are no plans for paid dues at this time. We'd consider it in the future only if the club moves toward events that involve food costs, require event insurance (like a larger fishing derby), private spot rentals, or hiring charters. For now, everything is free.

Have a suggestion about how the club should be run or structured? Do you have any ideas for future events or benefits for members? We'd love to hear it. Send us an email at oldcolonyanglers@gmail.com.
